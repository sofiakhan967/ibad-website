About Nikhil Sharma

                Nikhil Sharma,

                who has a YouTube channel by the name Mumbiker Nikhil,

                is one of the most popular YouTubers in India.

                At the time of writing, his vlogging channel had over 2.8 million subscribers.

                Additionally, the total views across all of his videos are a staggering 558 million!

                He was one of the firsts to make moto-vlogging famous in India.

                Here is something about the top moto-vlogger of India.

Nikhil Earnings & Income

According to a popular statistics website Social Blade,
Mumbiker Nikhil makes anywhere between Rs. 3,85,000 to Rs.
61,32,000 per month just from ads that appear before the video plays.
His earnings from brand endorsements and affiliates are not known.
He does brand endorsements on Instagram as well, where he has over 900,000 followers.
In addition, he has his own clothing line by the name LabelMN.
He sells his merchandise, which includes t-shirts, caps, bags and other accessories, online.

Mumbiker Early Career

Nikhil worked as a flight attendant in Qatar Airways till 2012.
While at it, he lived in Doha, Qatar. However,
his father’s demise made him come back to Mumbai,
where he then looked after his father’s business.
He tried his luck in acting but apart from a role in Emotional Atyachaar,
however, he couldn’t bag bigger roles and later shifted his focus to YouTube.
He lives with his mother in an apartment in Powai, according to one his YouTube videos.
His sister reportedly works as a graphics designer with Red Chillies Entertainment.
More about his family is not known.
